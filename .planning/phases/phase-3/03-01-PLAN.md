<task type="auto">
  <name>Task 1: Create server-side health score calculator</name>
  <files>server/src/utils/healthCalculator.ts</files>
  <action>
Create healthCalculator.ts with weighted multi-factor health score algorithm:

1. **Factor Weights** (per CONTEXT.md):
   - Activity: 0.30
   - Diet: 0.25
   - Age: 0.20
   - Weight: 0.15
   - Symptoms: 0.10

2. **Activity Score Calculation**:
   - Fetch 7-day activities for pet
   - Calculate avgDailyMinutes vs target based on species/weight
   - Species base minutes: dog=60, cat=30, bird=20, rabbit=30
   - Formula: `min(avgDailyMinutes / targetMinutes, 1) * 100`

3. **Diet Score Calculation**:
   - Fetch 7-day diet entries for pet
   - Calculate calories vs target (weight * species multiplier)
   - Species multipliers: dog=30kcal/lb, cat=20kcal/lb
   - Penalize both over and under-eating

4. **Age Score Calculation**:
   - Use pet.birthDate to calculate age in years
   - Optimal age range per species gets score of 100
   - Younger/older pets get progressively lower scores

5. **Weight Score Calculation**:
   - Use pet.weight from most recent entry or current
   - Compare to ideal weight range for species
   - Both underweight and overweight reduce score

6. **Symptom Score Calculation**:
   - Fetch 30-day symptoms
   - Severity weights: mild=0.3, moderate=0.6, severe=1.0
   - Frequency impact: more symptoms = lower score
   - Formula: `100 - (symptomPenalty * 10)`

7. **Export functions**:
   - `calculateHealthScore(pet, activities, diets, symptoms): HealthScoreResult`
   - `calculateActivityScore(activities, species, weight): number`
   - `calculateDietScore(diets, species, weight): number`
   - `calculateAgeScore(birthDate, species): number`
   - `calculateWeightScore(weight, species): number`
   - `calculateSymptomScore(symptoms): number`
</action>
